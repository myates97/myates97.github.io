All Caring Together

An on-campus organization at UNC Chapel Hill. ACT’s members promote love and kindness on UNC Chapel Hill’s campus through volunteering in the local area, hosting campus-wide events, and partaking in random acts of kindness.

A Simple Act of Kindness Goes a Long Way

A simple smile. A pat on the shoulder. A thoughtful complement. Every act of kindness sets off a chain reaction as each person passes on a kind act to others throughout their day. Our goal is to spread love and joy around UNC Chapel Hill’s campus, especially at a university where classes are challenging and stressful. Think of simple ways you can be kind to others in your everyday life!

"Kind words can be short and easy to speak, but their echos are truly endless"

-Mother Teresa
